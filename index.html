Welcome Readers!

When it comes time to escape the every day mundane activities of life, one of the first places I turn to is between the pages of a book. Ever since I was little my mother would read story books to me and it fascinated me the ways you could travel to other worlds without moving a single inch. This website is a resource to facts, recommendations, and more for book related things!

Benefits of Reading and why you should pick up a book:

  • Reading improves your critical thinking skills, without even knowing it, our brains provide different perspectives and solutions to the characters, situations and problems that arise in a story.

  • Reading can also enhance your vocabulary and conversation skills. Learning new words and their meanings help with understanding the world around you.

  • Picking up a book before bed can help reduce stress and improve your sleep. Reading helps distract from the problems of the day, even if it is non-fiction/factual. Studies show reading is more effective at reducing stress than going for a walk or listening to music.

  • Visualizing scenes, characters, and details from a story engages the brain’s sensory regions, enhancing memory retention through vivid mental imagery.

  • Reading different stories and perspectives helps with viewing things in a different way but it also provides a sense of support and belonging—particularly for those who may consider themselves somewhat of a "black sheep". One of the things reading offers is relatability and a reminder that we are not the first or only person to feel a certain way. Whether fiction or nonfiction, stories help us to process our own experiences- to feel seen and understood when this may be something missing in our own lives.
